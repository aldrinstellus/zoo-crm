About Us

Kidzee Pre-school

Kidzee Pre-school is a leading chain of Pre-schools located in India and Nepal backed by Zee Learn Limited. With more than two decades of experience in the Pre-school industry, Kidzee has nurtured more than 1.5m+ children throughout India. Being one of the largest Pre-schools in India. Kidzee has 2,500+ centres in 600+ cities in India and Nepal.

Kidzee Advantage

Kidzee is a leader and a trendsetter in Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE). Our business partners’ passion and drive are vital for our brand’s success.

Our Business Partners don't just take the name, they also take our pedagogy- PéNTEMiND, guidelines to teach, prescribes books/plays to be followed and so on to the ground.

Starting a Pre-school requires a strategic researching, planning, designing, and organising. Kidzee Professional research, makes this journey easier and rewarding for all our Business Partners.

Secret to Success is Service and Support. We provide pre-opening and operational support apart from the regular support that is given additional to all systems and process.

FAQs

Are the educators at Kidzee qualified and experienced?

Yes, Kidzee educators are certified professionals with extensive experience in early childhood education. We maintain a low teacher-student ratio to ensure personalized attention.

What sets Kidzee's curriculum apart from other preschools?

Kidzee's curriculum is designed to provide a well-rounded education, emphasizing creativity, critical thinking, and emotional intelligence. It's tailored to each age group and encourages experiential learning.

Can I tour the Kidzee Preschool facility before enrolling my child?

Yes, Kidzee encourages parents to visit our facilities to get a firsthand look at our nurturing environment. Please contact your local Kidzee center to schedule a tour.

How can parents stay involved in their child's education at Kidzee?

Kidzee promotes parent-teacher collaboration through regular meetings, progress updates, and various involvement opportunities to keep parents informed and engaged.

What is the process for admission to Kidzee Preschool?

Admission procedures may vary by location. To inquire about admissions, visit our website, or contact the nearest Kidzee center directly.

What advantages does enrolling my child in preschool offer, and when should they get started?

Preschool offers numerous benefits, including socialization, early learning skills, and cognitive development. It's recommended to start preschool between the ages of 2 to 3 years, but it can vary based on individual readiness.

What form of educational philosophy does Kidzee Preschool follow?

Kidzee Preschool follows a child-centric educational philosophy that focuses on holistic development. Our approach combines play-based learning, creativity, and a nurturing environment to foster a love for learning.

How does Kidzee help prepare children for the transition to primary school?

Kidzee's programs are designed to ensure a smooth transition to primary school by building essential skills in literacy, numeracy, social interaction, and emotional development.
