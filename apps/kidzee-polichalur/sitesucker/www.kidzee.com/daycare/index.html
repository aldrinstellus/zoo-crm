DayCare

Home |Programmes |Daycare

A home away from home for your child!

We understand that the early years in the child’s life are important for the overall development. The primary role of parents in these years is to provide their children with an environment that is loving, caring, fun-filled and one which provides happy experiences. As these aspects play a very important role in fostering the developmental areas, like, cognitive development, physical development, socio-emotional development, creative development , and language development. Our objective is to create a home away from home for them to feel safe, secure and happy.

Take a walkthrough of a day at Kidzee Daycare Facility:

  • Freshen up
  • Lunch
  • Nap time
  • Academics and academic assitance.(Home work)
  • Inter-intra personal skills
  • Indoor activities
  • Snacks
  • Outdoor Activities
  • Edutainment

FAQs

Is Kidzee only available in certain regions or cities?

Kidzee has a wide presence across various locations with the presence of 750+ cities. To find a Kidzee Preschool near you, please use our school locator on the website.

What age groups do Kidzee programs cater to?

Kidzee offers programs for children aged 1.5 years to 5.5 years, including Playgroup, Nursery, and Kindergarten.

What is Kidzee Preschool's philosophy of early education?

testKidzee believes in providing a holistic learning experience that nurtures a child's cognitive, emotional, physical, and social development through age-appropriate, play-based learning.

How can I find Kidzee daycare near me?

Parents can find out Kidzee Day Care through Google Website,Kidzee Webiste, Kidzee Day care centresContact Number and Helpline Numbers .

At what age my child is eligible for daycare admission??

Children from 1.5years - 06 years will be eligible for Day care admissions.
