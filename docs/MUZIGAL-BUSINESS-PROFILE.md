## 8. Key Observations for CRM Design

1. **Multi-age system**: Foundation (3-6), Beginners (7+), Adults — CRM needs age-appropriate handling
2. **Multi-instrument**: 6+ core instruments at Borewell, 20+ across platform — course/instrument categorization needed
3. **Multiple levels per instrument**: Foundation → Beginners → Intermediate → Advanced — progression tracking
4. **Franchise model**: System should be replicable across locations
5. **Parent vs student**: Young students (3-6) → parent is the contact, not the student
6. **Certification tracking**: Trinity, ABRSM, Rockschool — exam/grade tracking needed
7. **Shop integration**: Instrument sales as a revenue stream — potential CRM module
8. **Teacher management**: 400+ teachers across platform — scheduling, availability, skills
9. **Operating hours**: Mon-Sat 11AM-9PM, Sun 11AM-2PM — scheduling within these windows
10. **Payment**: Razorpay integration already exists — can leverage for fee management
11. **Royalty structure**: 80/20 split — financial reporting needed
12. **Performance focus**: Recitals, stage performances, band formation — event management module potential
